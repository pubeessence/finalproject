keybros

For all your mecahnical keyboard needs 💻

Founded in the bustling Lower Mainland of Vancouver, Keybros began with a simple yet ambitious mission: to make mechanical keyboard building and customization accessible to everyone. The brainchild of two close friends who shared a passion for technology, design, and functionality, Keybros quickly established itself as a hub for both newcomers and seasoned enthusiasts in the mechanical keyboard world.


The Origin Story 🐣

The mechanical keyboard community has long been admired for its creativity and innovation. However, high costs and the steep learning curve often served as barriers for those interested in joining the hobby. The founders of Keybros noticed this gap and set out to create a solution. Drawing from their own experiences of frustration with overpriced kits and confusing guides, they envisioned a company that would simplify the process without compromising on quality. Their journey started humbly in Vancouver, where they pooled their resources to design and source budget-friendly yet high-quality keyboard components. They began offering DIY kits and pre-built options, ensuring that anyone, regardless of skill level or budget, could enjoy the satisfaction of owning a personalized mechanical keyboard.


A Passion for the Future 📈

Keybros continues to push boundaries in the mechanical keyboard space. The founders remain hands-on, constantly seeking feedback and improving their offerings. Their ultimate goal is to demystify the world of mechanical keyboards, ensuring that more people can experience the joy of creating something both functional and personal. Through affordability, education, and community, Keybros has turned a niche hobby into an accessible passion for many. Their journey is a testament to what can be achieved when creativity, inclusivity, and dedication come together.
